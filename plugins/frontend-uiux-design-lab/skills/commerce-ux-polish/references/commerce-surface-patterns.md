# Commerce Surface Patterns

## Listings And Grids

- Keep image, title, rating, final price, and delivery confidence within a short scan path.
- Make discount treatment secondary to the real price.
- Keep wishlist, compare, and quick view optional and visually quiet.
- Preserve filter and sort context when users move between list and detail.

## Product Detail Pages

- Keep gallery, variant selection, stock status, delivery estimate, and CTA closely grouped.
- Make return, warranty, and financing information easy to find without burying the CTA.
- Use sticky action treatment on mobile when the purchase area scrolls away.

## Cart

- Show editable line items, subtotal, savings, and delivery expectations without surprise.
- Let users fix issues without losing their position in the flow.
- Keep cross-sell modules clearly secondary to checkout.

## Checkout

- Show progress and reassure users about payment safety and order review.
- Use summary blocks that remain legible on small screens.
- Surface errors next to the fields that need correction.

## Mobile Notes

- Treat sticky purchase actions as utilities, not as oversized banners.
- Keep filter drawers fast, reversible, and easy to exit.
- Watch for stacked badges and price blocks that become visually noisy on narrow widths.
