# Form Patterns

## Before Submit

- Show field intent clearly.
- Validate progressively when it helps.
- Keep high-risk inputs near supporting explanation.

## Error Handling

- Place errors near the source.
- State what is wrong and how to fix it.
- Preserve entered data after validation errors.

## Multi-Step Flows

- Show progress.
- Keep one primary action per step.
- Let users go back without losing everything.

## Trust

- Explain why personal or financial data is required.
- Keep success and verification messages calm and specific.
