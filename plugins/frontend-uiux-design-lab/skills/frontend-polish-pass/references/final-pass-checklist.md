# Final Pass Checklist

- The main action is obvious within a quick scan.
- Section spacing feels intentional rather than accidental.
- The page survives mobile widths without awkward stacking.
- Icons, badges, and buttons share a consistent visual weight.
- Empty, loading, success, and error states exist and match the system.
- Sticky elements do not cover important content.
- CTA copy is specific and confident.
- Contrast and focus states still hold up after polish changes.
- Decorative accents support the hierarchy instead of competing with it.
