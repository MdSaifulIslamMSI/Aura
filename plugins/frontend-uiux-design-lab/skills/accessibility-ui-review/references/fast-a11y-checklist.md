# Fast Accessibility Checklist

## Structure

- Headings follow a sensible outline.
- Landmarks and regions help with page navigation.
- Buttons are buttons and links are links.

## Controls

- Every control has a visible label or accessible name.
- Focus is visible and follows a logical order.
- Menus, dialogs, tabs, and popovers work from the keyboard.

## Visuals

- Important states are not conveyed by color alone.
- Contrast remains readable for text, icons, and controls.
- Text still works at zoomed sizes and narrow widths.

## Motion And Feedback

- Reduced motion is respected for major transitions.
- Async feedback tells the user what is happening.
- Errors are announced close to the relevant field or action.
